Age of Empires 2 Counter List

There wasn’t a centralized source for counters in AoE2, so I decided to make one. It pulls from various sources, ranging from the wiki, Spirit of the Law, various AoK threads, and Steam discussions. For the icons, I went through and downloaded each image from the wiki, photoshoped out the black background, and uploaded it to imgur. Looking back, I wish I knew python so I didn’t have to do it manually. Since it’s release, it’s accumulated over 9,000 unique views on Steam.
