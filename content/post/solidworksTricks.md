## Custom Binds

Key             | Action
---             | ---
`e`             | Regular extrude
`Alt` + `e`     | Subtrack extrude
`Alt` + `F`     | Fillet/Round (feature)
`Shift` + `F`   | Chamfer (feature)
`H`             | Hole Wizard
`Alt` + `H`     | Advaanced Holes
`Shift` + `H`   | Thread
`Alt` + `1`     | Linear Pattern
`Alt` + `2`     | Circular Pattern
`Alt` + `3`     | Mirror
`Alt` + `4`     | Fill Pattern
`S`             | Sketch
`L`             | Line
`R`             | Corner Rectangle
